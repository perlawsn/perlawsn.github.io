Past events
A general meeting on PerLa project is scheduled on Thursday 10th December 2009.
The goal of this meeting is to explain the state of art of the project and to schedule future works. The event will be valid as project seminar for the Technologies for Informations Systems course.
The following presentations will be discussed:
  • F.A.Schreiber: Overview on PerLa project
  • R. Camplani: Short introduction to PerLa middleware
  • G. Rota: FPC Factory and devices self-description
  • D. Viganò: Low Level Query Executor architecture
  • R. Camplani: A proposal for the Low Level C library architecture
  • C. Cappiello: Energy-aware data compression
  • PerLa Staff: Future works (Context management, Intelligent protocols, ...)

The slides used during the meeting are available here.
In June 2009 we presented PerLa project at the SMScom ERC Meeting in Como.
In March 2009 we partecipated to the 5th International Workshop on Sensor Networks and Systems for Pervasive Computing (PerSeNS 2009), presenting: Quality- and Energy-Aware Data Compression by Aggregation in WSN Data Streams (Cappiello C., Schreiber F.A.).
